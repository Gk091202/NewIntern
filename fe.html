💡 Study Tips for FE

Essential strategies to excel in your first year

Time Management

Create a study schedule and stick to it. Balance theory and practical sessions effectively.

Study Groups

Form study groups with classmates to discuss difficult concepts and solve problems together.

Ask for Help

Don't hesitate to ask professors and seniors when you're stuck. Clarify doubts immediately.

Regular Reading

Make reading a daily habit. Go through lecture notes and reference books regularly.

Practice Coding

Write programs daily. Practice coding problems and understand logic building step by step.

Stay Healthy

Maintain a healthy lifestyle with proper sleep, exercise, and nutrition for better focus.
